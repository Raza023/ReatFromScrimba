How to get the most of this course?

1) The easist way to code is the hard way.
2) Learn by doing.
3) Avoid "Tutorial watching hectics"
4) You are not being judged, so it's okay if you make mistakes.

Points to remember from "Make it stick" book:
1) Active retrieval. 
    (Keep on reading and practicing what you have already learnt, Don't just watch and assume that you have understood.)
2) Spaced repetition. 
    (Take breaks and get back. You can work on 20-20-20 formula. i.e, 20 mins of work and then look 20 feets away for 20 seconds.)
3) Varied applications. 
    (Apply what you have learnt in varity of ways.)
